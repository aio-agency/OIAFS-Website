About OIAFS

A Cultural Crown Jewel of African Heritage Month in Florida

What began as OAFS has blossomed into OIAFS, an international phenomenon celebrating African creativity and culture. Over 9 consecutive years, we have grown from a grassroots community event to a global platform attracting designers and performers from Africa, the UK, and beyond.
